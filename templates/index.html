Sushruta

(Father of Surgery)

Sushruta

Time Period

Circa 600 BCE

Major Contributions

Authored Sushruta Samhita, pioneered plastic surgery, described 300 surgical procedures, 120 surgical instruments

Legacy

Father of Surgery, established foundational principles of Ayurvedic surgery

Charaka

(Father of Medicine)

Charaka

Time Period

Circa 300 BCE

Major Contributions

Authored Charaka Samhita, established principles of internal medicine, emphasized prevention over cure

Legacy

Father of Ayurvedic Medicine, foundational text remains essential for Ayurvedic education

Vagbhata

(Compiler of Knowledge)

Vagbhata

Time Period

Circa 6th-7th Century CE

Major Contributions

Authored Ashtanga Hridayam and Ashtanga Sangraha, synthesized previous Ayurvedic knowledge

Legacy

Created comprehensive texts that integrated previous Ayurvedic works, made knowledge more accessible

Nagarjuna

(Alchemist & Physician)

Nagarjuna

Time Period

Circa 2nd-3rd Century CE

Major Contributions

Pioneer of Rasashastra (alchemy), developed mercurial preparations, advanced pharmaceutical techniques

Legacy

Father of Indian Alchemy, revolutionized Ayurvedic pharmacy with mineral preparations

Madhavakara

(Diagnosis Specialist)

Madhavakara

Time Period

Legacy

Master of Diagnosis, created comprehensive disease classification system still used today

Bhavamisra

(Encyclopedic Scholar)

Bhavamisra

Time Period

Circa 16th Century CE

Legacy
